Wife-selling

Wife-seling is an act that involves a husband auctioning off and selling their wives
for not only money, but also as a way of divorve between the seller-husband and
the wife. The buyer on the other hand automatically gets married to the wife that
is being sold. This was a popular method of self divorce and self marriage during
the 17th century at England.

Fishbone Diagram

The fishbone diagram is quite different from the 1st two. Unlike mind mapping
that helped us find which solution might be best, or the 5 whys that served as
a way to peel away the problem to find the root cause, the fishbone diagram
serves as more of a cause and effect technique.

Starting from the first fish bone (the one near the tail at the very end), it states
causes connected to financial problems. The middle bone states problems
about marriage that led to the effect, while the bone nearest to the head (the
effect) are causes connected to divorce.

Solutions

After using various brain storming methods, my group has come up with
possible solutions that couldve made crucial impacts to our chosen issue.

Education

We can all agree that education is one of the most important and crucial
factors that shapes us up, making us what we are now. Because of the
patriarcy and multiple misogynistic gender stereotypes during the 17th
century, women did not have any proper access to education.

Empowerment

Without the rights to education, empowerment and independence played
a crucial role during the 17th century to these women in order to survive.
Showing support can lead to encouragement and empowerment to one
another.

Spreading information

Women bringing awareness to eachouther about the dehumanizing truth
about wife selling. Making the government hear women's voices is a good
way to make them acknowledge their discrimination against women and for
the government to start having laws to protect women. Fighting for their
rights can show that women are more capable than the mindset everyone
had towards them. Having access to education without discrimination, can
lead to empowered women and people who can solve the problems of
their marriage without having to sell anyone. Making people aware with
being human and leading them to disagreeing with selling people, will
decrease the acts of wife-selling and the support for it.

Conclusion

With all those facts stated above, me and my group came to conclusion
and picked out a solution from the mind map. Our chosen solutino is:
"Challenge/debate their ideas and stereotypes about women by protesting
about the ethics of wife-selling and flaws". We all decided that this would
be the best, most impactful and realistic solution that couldve helped
solving our chosen problem. Even though the other solutions stated on
the mind map wouldve also helped with putting a halt to wife selling,
not all of them are exactly realistic and applicable to the timeline
where our problem happened. Our group made sure to apply all the
possible solutions in the timeline, and came to conclusion that our
chosen one would be the best to not only raise awareness, but also
let peoples voices be heard.
